About NIELIT

NIELIT is a premier institute under the Ministry of Electronics & IT, Government of India. It provides world-class training in computer science, IT, electronics, software development, and related technology fields.

Courses Offered

  • O Level – Foundation Course in Computer Application
  • A Level – Advanced Diploma in Computer Application
  • Programming in C, C++, Java, Python
  • Web Designing & Development
  • Basic & Advanced Digital Marketing
  • IoT, Robotics & AI Introduction

Our Faculty

Our faculty team consists of certified trainers, IT professionals, engineers, and experienced educators who help students gain real-world technical skills.
